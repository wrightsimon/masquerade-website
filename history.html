History of the Talverton Masquerade

The origin of the this great ball stretches back all the way to the creation of the Sencoss bloodline. What seems like a millenia ago, an elderly alchemist named Egyed Sencoss was experimenting to find agelessness for him and his grandson. When his home in the Talverton province entered into famine a stranger convinced him to experiment with blood. Egyed and his sons trapped an angel, and using her blood, Egyed found what he was looking for. With his new decoction, Egyed gave his family line, and a specific few others access to immortality at the cost of an unnatural thirst for blood. Thus, the vampire race was born.

Since their rebirth, the Sencoss's have been hosting a masquerade ball, inviting their favored family lines as well as prominent citizens of the surrounding areas. There has always been rumor of the Sencoss's dark secrets, but the reputation of the ball is enough to bring even the most skeptical of minds. Every year the Sencoss's push for a larger amount of attendees, this year they hope for an amount in the thousands. A brief history of attendees can be seen below.
